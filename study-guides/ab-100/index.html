AB-100 · Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect — Study Guide & Quiz

Design, build, and deploy agentic-first, AI-powered business solutions across Dynamics 365, Power Platform, Copilot Studio, and Microsoft Foundry.

Level: Expert 3 skill domains 20 practice questions Pass score: 700/1000 Last verified 14 Jun 2026

1 · Plan AI-powered business solutions 25–30% of exam

Analyze requirements, set the overall AI strategy, and justify investment through ROI for agentic business solutions.

Analyze requirements for AI-powered business solutions

  • Assess the use of agents in task automation, data analytics, and decision-making
  • Review data for grounding — accuracy, relevance, timeliness, cleanliness, and availability
  • Organize business-solution data so it is available for other AI systems

Design the overall AI strategy

  • Apply the AI adoption process from the Cloud Adoption Framework for Azure
  • Design a multi-agent solution using Microsoft 365 Copilot, Copilot Studio, and Microsoft Foundry
  • Decide when to build custom agents vs. extend Microsoft 365 Copilot, and when to create custom AI models or small language models
  • Develop use cases for prebuilt agents; define rules and constraints for Copilot Studio / Foundry / Foundry Tools
  • Provide prompt engineering guidelines and a prompt library; include the elements of the Microsoft AI Center of Excellence
  • Design AI solutions that span multiple Dynamics 365 apps

Evaluate costs and benefits

  • Select ROI criteria including total cost of ownership (TCO)
  • Create an ROI analysis for a proposed AI solution for a business process
  • Analyze whether to build, buy, or extend AI components
Exam tip: Know the decision tree — extend M365 Copilot for productivity scenarios, Copilot Studio for low-code custom/agentic flows, and Microsoft Foundry for pro-code custom models and orchestration.

2 · Design AI-powered business solutions 25–30% of exam

Architect the agents, their extensibility, and the configuration of prebuilt AI features across the Microsoft business-applications stack.

Design AI and agents for business solutions

  • Design task agents, autonomous agents, and prompt-and-response agents
  • Design Copilot for Dynamics 365 customer experience & service — business terms, customizations, and Sales connectors
  • Design agents for Dynamics 365 Contact Center channels
  • Design topics (including fallback), agent flows, and prompt actions in Copilot Studio
  • Choose between standard NLP, conversational language understanding, or generative AI orchestration in Copilot Studio
  • Apply the Power Platform Well-Architected Framework to intelligent application workloads
  • Propose Foundry Tools and code-first generative pages / agent feeds

Design extensibility of AI solutions

  • Design AI solutions using custom models in Microsoft Foundry
  • Design agent extensibility in Copilot Studio, including with the Model Context Protocol (MCP)
  • Design agents that automate tasks via Computer Use in Copilot Studio
  • Design agent behaviors — reasoning and voice mode
  • Optimize design with agents in Microsoft 365, including Teams and SharePoint

Orchestrate configuration for prebuilt agents and apps

  • Orchestrate AI features in Dynamics 365 finance & supply chain and in customer experience & service apps
  • Configure M365 Copilot for Sales and Copilot for Service
  • Propose Power Platform AI features, including the AI hub
  • Design interoperability of finance & operations agent chats with additional knowledge sources
Exam tip: Expect to differentiate agent types (task vs. autonomous vs. prompt-and-response) and to pick the right orchestration mode in Copilot Studio for a given scenario.

3 · Deploy AI-powered business solutions 40–45% of exam

The heaviest domain: monitor and tune solutions, test them, run ALM, and govern responsible-AI, security, and compliance.

Analyze, monitor, and tune

  • Recommend the process and tools for monitoring agents
  • Analyze backlog and user feedback of AI / agent usage
  • Apply AI-based tools to identify issues and perform tuning
  • Monitor agent performance and metrics; interpret telemetry data for performance and model tuning

Manage testing

  • Recommend the process and metrics to test agents; create validation criteria for custom AI models
  • Validate effective Copilot prompt best practices
  • Design end-to-end test scenarios across multiple Dynamics 365 apps
  • Build a strategy for creating test cases by using Copilot

Design the ALM process

  • Design ALM for data used in AI models and agents
  • Design ALM for Copilot Studio agents, connectors, and actions
  • Design ALM for Microsoft Foundry agents and for custom AI models
  • Design ALM for AI in Dynamics 365 finance & supply chain and in customer experience & service apps

Responsible AI, security, governance, risk & compliance

  • Design security and governance for agents, and model security
  • Analyze solution and AI vulnerabilities and mitigations, including prompt manipulation
  • Review the solution for adherence to responsible AI principles
  • Validate data residency and movement compliance
  • Design access controls on grounding data and model tuning; design audit trails for changes to models and data
Exam tip: This domain is the largest — prioritize it. Be fluent in responsible-AI safeguards (prompt-injection defense, data residency, audit trails) and ALM across Copilot Studio and Foundry.
